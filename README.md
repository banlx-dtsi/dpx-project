# dpx-project
DPX Solution
================================

DPX Solution reduces the complexity and improve responsiveness of business processes. Thanks to its modularity, you can start with few features and  then activate other modules when needed.

DPX Solution includes the following modules :

* Customer Relationship Management
* Sales management
* Financial and cost management
* Human Resource Management
* Project Management
* Inventory and Supply Chain Management
* Production Management
* Multi-company, multi-currency and multi-lingual
